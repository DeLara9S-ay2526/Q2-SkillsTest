Admissions Overview

OB Montessori Greenhills welcomes applications from families who share our vision of education that nurtures independence, creativity, and a love for learning.

Process

Our admissions process is designed to ensure that OB Montessori Greenhills is the right fit for your child and family. We welcome applications throughout the year, with priority given to applications received during our main enrollment period.

1. Inquiry and School Tour

Begin by contacting our Admissions Office to schedule a campus tour. This will give you an opportunity to see our facilities, meet our staff, and learn more about our Montessori approach.

2. Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with all required documents and the application fee. Applications can be submitted online or in person at our Admissions Office.

3. Assessment and Interview

Applicants will be scheduled for an assessment appropriate to their age level. Parents will also be invited for an interview to discuss their educational goals and expectations.

4. Acceptance and Enrollment

Families will be notified of the admissions decision within two weeks of completing the assessment and interview. Upon acceptance, parents will receive an enrollment package with instructions for completing the registration process.
